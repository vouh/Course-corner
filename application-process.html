KUCCPS Application Process

1

Eligibility Check

  • Ensure you meet the minimum KCSE requirements for your desired program
  • Verify that you sat the KCSE exam between 2000 and 2023
2

Create an Account

  • Visit the KUCCPS Student's Portal (students.kuccps.net)
  • Create an account with your personal details and KCSE results
3

Log In to the Portal

  • Use your account credentials to log in to the KUCCPS Student's Portal
4

Select Programs

  • Browse through available programs at universities, colleges, and TVET institutions
  • Select preferred programs based on KCSE grades and interests
5

Submit Application

  • Complete the application form with all required details
  • Review your application thoroughly before submission
6

Make Payment

  • Follow payment instructions on the portal
  • Ensure you receive payment confirmation
