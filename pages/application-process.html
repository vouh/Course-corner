Your step-by-step guide to applying for university placement

1

Eligibility Check

  • Ensure you meet the minimum KCSE requirements for your desired program (usually C+ and above for degree programs)
  • Verify that you sat the KCSE exam between 2000 and
  • Have your KCSE index number and birth certificate details ready
2

Create an Account

  • Visit the official KUCCPS Student's Portal at students.kuccps.net
  • Click on "Create Account" and fill in your personal details
  • Enter your KCSE index number and year of examination
  • Create a strong password and verify your email address
3

Log In to the Portal

  • Use your KCSE index number and password to log in
  • Verify that your personal details and KCSE results are correct
  • If you encounter login issues, use the "Forgot Password" option
4

Select Programs

  • Browse through available programs at universities, colleges, and TVET institutions
  • Filter programs based on your cluster points and subject requirements
  • Select up to 6 program choices in order of preference
  • Consider factors like location, cost, and career prospects
5

Submit Application

  • Complete the application form with all required details
  • Review your program choices carefully before submission
  • Ensure all information is accurate ‚Äì you may not be able to edit later
  • Save a copy of your application for reference
6

Make Payment

  • Follow payment instructions provided on the portal (M-Pesa paybill available)
  • The application fee varies based on the type of application
  • Keep your payment confirmation receipt/SMS
  • Your application is only complete after successful payment

Important: Always apply through the official KUCCPS portal (students.kuccps.net). Beware of fraudulent websites. KUCCPS will never ask for your password or personal banking details outside the official portal.

Need Help?

If you encounter any issues or have questions during the application process, our support team and KUCCPS help desk are here to assist you.
