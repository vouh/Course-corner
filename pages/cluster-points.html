How Cluster Points Work

Each cluster for KUCCPS courses has 4 major required subjects. These subjects are used in the formula to calculate cluster points. If a student did not take or study a required standalone subject for a specific cluster (e.g., Music for Cluster 18 or Physics for Cluster 4), the cluster points are automatically set to zero for that cluster.

This scoring system is competitive and helps students get into the institutions of their choice. Every university has specific cluster points requirements for each course. This system applies to students who have scored a C+ and above.

Important Note: Cluster point requirements may vary by university and program. Please check specific university requirements for accurate information.
