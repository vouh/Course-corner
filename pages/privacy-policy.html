Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 1, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how CourseCorner collects, uses, stores, and protects your personal information.

By using our platform, you consent to the practices described here.

2. Information We Collect

a) Information You Provide

We collect information you voluntarily provide, including:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Course progress and submissions
  • Payment information (processed securely by third-party providers)

b) Information We Do NOT Collect Automatically

CourseCorner does not collect:

  • Device information
  • IP address
  • Browser type
  • Usage statistics (pages visited, time spent, etc.)

We only collect information that you intentionally provide.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Deliver courses and certificates
  • Provide customer support
  • Improve platform functionality
  • Send important updates or notifications
  • Maintain platform security

We do not sell your personal information.

4. Sharing Your Information

We may share your data with:

  • Payment processors
  • Cloud hosting providers
  • Customer support tools
  • Legal authorities (only when required by law)

All third-party partners must follow data protection standards.

5. Data Storage & Security

  • Your data is stored on secure servers.
  • We use encryption, access controls, and monitoring to protect your information.
  • While no system is 100% secure, we take all reasonable measures to safeguard your data.

6. Your Rights

You may:

  • Access your personal data
  • Update or correct your information
  • Request account-related assistance
  • Opt out of marketing communications

Requests can be made through our support channels.

7. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary for:

  • Providing services
  • Legal compliance
  • Resolving disputes

After that, data is securely deleted.

8. Children’s Privacy

CourseCorner does not knowingly collect data from children under 16 without parental consent.

9. International Data Transfers

If your data is processed outside Kenya, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically.

Continued use of the platform means you accept the updated version.
